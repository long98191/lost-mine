# Qelline Alderleaf

## Story so far

Qelline Alderleaf is the owner of the [[Alderleaf Farm]]. A wise female halfling of forty-five, she was kind enough to offer you a place to sleep in the hayloft should the [[Stonehill Inn]] proves too costly.

Qelline's son, Carp, a wannabe adventurer, revealed that he has found a secret tunnel in the woods next to [[Tresendar Manor]]. He had seen bandits walking around the area, "up to no good".

Qelline has a longtime druid friend named [[Reidoth]], who might offer helpful insights should you need to consult him. He has recently set out to the town of [[Thundertree]], northwest of Phandalin.
