# Reidoth

## Story so far

Reidoth the druid is a friend of [[Qelline Alderleaf]], who knows every inch of the land around Phandalin. He might be of help to you should you decide to seek guidance from him.

He has recently set out to the town of [[Thundertree]], northwest of Phandalin.
