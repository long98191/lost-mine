# Sildar Hallwinter

## Story so far

Sildar Hallwinter is a member of the [[Lords' Alliance]], who was tasked with ensuring the law and order of Phandalin. He was ambushed and abducted on the way to Phandalin alongside his close friend [[Gundren Rockseeker]], who shared his information on the [[Wave Echo Cave]] with him. Sildar was rescued by you, but the location of Gundren is still unknown.

After being rescued from the goblin band, Sildar let you know of a few pieces of important information:
- The three Rockseeker brothers (Gundren, Tharden, and Nundro) recently located an entrance to the long-lost [[Wave Echo Cave]], site of the mines of the Phandelver's Pact.
- Klarg, the bugbear who leads this goblin band, had orders to waylay Gundren. Sildar heard from the goblins that a figure called the [[Black Spider]] sent word that the dwarf was to be brought to him. Sildar doesn't know who or what the Black Spider is.
- Gundren had a map showing the secret location of Wave  Echo Cave, but the goblins took it when they captured him. Sildar believes that Klarg sent the map and the dwarf to the chief of the Cragmaws at a place called Cragmaw Castle. Sildar doesn't know where that might be, but he suggests someone in Phandalin might know.

A stoic figure seeking to bring order to Phandalin, Sildar depises [[Harbin Wester]]'s ignorance of the [[Redbrands Ruffians]]' activities, and also believes that the reactivation of the Wave Echo Cave is crucial to bringing prosperity and stability to the mining town. To achieve that, he has tasked you with finding the [[Cragmaw Castle]], where Gundren and his map to the mine is allegedly held.
