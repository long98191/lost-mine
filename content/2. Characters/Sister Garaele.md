# Sister Garaele

## Story so far

Sister Garaele is a zealous young elf, and an acolyte of the [[Shrine of Luck]] in Phandalin. 

When you visited her, she was strewn with bandages, allegedly wounded after returning from an assignment out of town.

From the conversation with her, you found out that she was tasked with gathering information from a banshee named Agatha, located near the ruins of [[Conyberry]], about a spellbook belonging to a legendary mage named Bowgentle.  After hearing about your victory against the goblin raiders, she would like to ask for your help, bringing a jeweled silver comb as a gift to exchange for the information of the spellbook. In exchange for the task, she hinted to reward the party with access to a strong network of information spreading throughout the continent.
