# Linene Graywind

## Story so far

Linene Graywind - a sharp-tongued human woman of thirty-five - is the master of the [[Lionshield Coster]]'s post in Phandalin.  

Linene revealed that she was expecting a caravan from Phandalin, but was informed that it was raided, not knowing who was responsible. She also warned you that the Redbrands ruffians are trouble and advised you to avoid the Sleeping Giant tap house.

You returned the stolen goods to Linene, receiving gold as appreciation.
