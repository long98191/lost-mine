# Gundren Rockseeker

## Story so far

Gundren Rockseeker is a friend of [[Sildar Hallwinter]], who, together with his brothers Nundro and Tharden, discovered the location of the lost [[Wave Echo Cave]], the legendary home of the Forge of Spells.

Described by Sildar to be knowledgeable and resourceful, Gundren has been a prospector and treasure hunter since he was young. As he was preparing for the excavation of the Cave, he was ambushed and taken hostage. His whereabouts is still unknown, although his map to the Cave was allegedly taken to a place called [[Cragmaw Castle]].
