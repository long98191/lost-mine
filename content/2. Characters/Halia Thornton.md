# Halia Thornton

## Story so far

Halia Thornton is the guildmaster of the [[Phandalin's Miner Exchange]]. In her attempts to establish the Miner's Exchange as the closest thing the town has to a governing authority, she acts as more than a simple merchant. She also claims that she has access to a network of money pursuers who seeks to control the North through sheer wealth and influence.

 Halia has requested that you take Glasstaff out of the equation, in exchange of a good amount of money and access to the most powerful moneymakers the North have seen.
