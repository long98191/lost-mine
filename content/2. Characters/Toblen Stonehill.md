# Toblen Stonehill

## Story so far

The innkeeper of the [[Stonehill Inn]] is [[Toblen Stonehill]], a friendly young human male. Toblen is a native of the town of Triboar to the east of Phandalin. He came to Phandalin to prospect, but soon realized that he knew a lot more about running an inn than he did about mining.

Toblen is upset that the [Redbrands](Redbrands%20Ruffians.md) have been allowed to terrorize the town, and that [[Harbin Wester]], the townmaster, has done nothing to curtail them. However, he tries not to stir up trouble for fear that the Redbrands might retaliate against his wife and children.
