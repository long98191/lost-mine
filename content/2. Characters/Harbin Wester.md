# Harbin Wester

## Story so far

The current townmaster of Phandalin is a male human banker named Harbin Wester - a fat, pompous old fool. Completely intimidated by the [[Redbrands Ruffians]], he claims that they're "just a mercenary guild, and not all that much trouble, really."

Harbin complained about orcs raiders running loose on the Triboar Trail, near Wyvern Tor, a few days' journey east of Phandalin. He offered your party 100gp to take care of the problem.
