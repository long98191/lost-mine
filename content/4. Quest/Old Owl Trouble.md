# Old Owl Trouble

## Story so far

[[Daran Edermath]], a retired adventurer heard that mining prospectors coming to [[Old Owl Well]] - a ruin northeast of Phandalin - had been chased from the area by undead. He asked you to investigate the ruins, find out who's there and what they're up to. He gave you a ring of striding and springing to aid you with the quest, and hinted that he would put in a good word with the [[Order of the Gauntlet]], should anyone suits the characteristics and capability that they are looking for.
