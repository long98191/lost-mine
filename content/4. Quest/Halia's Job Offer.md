# Halia's Job Offer

## Story so far

You have heard [[Halia Thornton]], master of the [[Phandalin's Miner Exchange]], reveal that the [[Redbrands Ruffians]] likely have a base of operation somewhere close to town. She offered you 100gp to eliminate the Redbrand leader, whom she knows is called [[Glasstaff]], and bring her any information you can find at their headquarter - letters, missives, trade records, anything. She further offered access to a network of money pursuers who seeks to control the North through sheer wealth and influence, should your goals align with them.
