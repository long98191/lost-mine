# Finding Iarno

## Story so far

[[Sildar Hallwinter]]'s fellow [[Lords' Alliance]] member, [[Iarno Albrek]], has gone missing for a few months after being assigned to the Phandalin post. Sildar's sources said that Iarno disappeared while exploring the area  around [[Tresendar Manor]]. He asked you to investigate the manor and the surrounding area to find and bring back larno - or what's left of him, if something killed him. Sildar describes larno as "a short, darkbearded human wizard in his thirties."
