# Ruins of Thundertree

## Story so far

[[Qelline Alderleaf]] , owner of the [[Alderleaf Farm]], suggested that you meet with her longtime friend, a druid named [[Reidoth]], since "there's not an inch of the land he doesn't know". She told you that Reidoth recently set out for the ruins of a town called [[Thundertree]], just west of the [[Neverwinter Wood]]. The ruins are about fifty miles northwest of Phandalin, and she provides directions so the characters can easily find the place.
