# Tresendar Manor

## Story so far

More a castle than a house, Tresendar Manor stands at the east edge of town on a low hillside amid woods and thickets. The ancient manor has long been abandoned, and many people think that it is haunted - whispers emanating from the manor are often heard around town at night.

According to [[Sildar Hallwinter]], his fellow Lords' Alliance member [[Iarno Albrek]], has  disappeared while exploring the area around Tresendar Manor.
