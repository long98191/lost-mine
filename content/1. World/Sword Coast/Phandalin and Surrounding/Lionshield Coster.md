# Lionshield Coster

> Hanging above the front door of this modest trading post is a sign shaped like a wooden shield with a blue lion painted on it.

## Story so far

According to [[Toblen Stonehill]], you know that this building is a trading post owned by the Lionshields, a merchant company based in the city of Yartar, over a hundred miles to the east. They ship finished goods to Phandalin and other small settlements throughout the region, but this outpost has been hard hit by banditry.

The master of the post in Phandalin is a sharp-tongued human woman of thirty-five named [[Linene Graywind]]. She revealed that she was expecting a caravan from Phandalin, but was informed that it was raided, not knowing who was responsible. She also warned you that the Redbrands ruffians are trouble and advised you to avoid the Sleeping Giant tap house.

Linene keeps a supply of armor and weapons in the trading post, which are for sale to interested buyers, but won't sell to anyone she thinks might be a threat to the town.

You revealed that you have recovered the raided caravan from the goblins, and decided to return the stolen goods. For this, you received 50 gp.
