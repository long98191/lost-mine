# Triboar Trail

## Story so far

The Triboar Trail is a east-west running trail, connecting the High Road and [[Neverwinter]] to the swamp and mountain cities and settlements in the middle of Faerun.

It is currently overrun with orc raiders and goblin bands, who might have been responsible for the capture of [[Sildar Hallwinter]] and [[Gundren Rockseeker]]. [[Harbin Wester]] is looking for a stop to the orc crisis.
