# Cragmaw Castle

## Story so far

While captured by the goblin gang, [[Sildar Hallwinter]] heard of a place called [[Cragmaw Castle]], where [Gundren](Gundren%20Rockseeker)'s map to the [[Wave Echo Cave]] was taken. The location of the Castle is unknown, though Sildar is trying his hardest to take down the castle in an attempt to find his dwarven and to retrieve the location of the Cave.
