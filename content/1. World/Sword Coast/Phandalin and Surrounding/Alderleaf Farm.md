# Alderleaf Farm

## Story so far

The farm owner is a wise female halfling of forty-five named [[Qelline Alderleaf]]. She is a kind host, and offered to let you sleep in her hayloft if you cannot afford a bed at the [[Stonehill Inn]]. 

Her son, Carp, is a spirited and precocious halfling lad of ten years. He is enchanted by the idea of being an adventurer and says that he was playing in the woods near [[Tresendar Manor]] when he found a secret tunnel in a thicket. A couple of "big ugly bandits" came out of the tunnel when he was there, and he thinks that the bandits have a secret lair under the old manor house. He offered to take you to the tunnel, or at least provide the direction to the location.

Upon hearing you are working with [Sildar](Sildar%20Hallwinter.md), Qelline suggested that you meet with her longtime friend, a druid named [[Reidoth]], since "there's not an inch of the land he doesn't know". She told you that Reidoth recently set out for the ruins of a town called [[Thundertree]], just west of the [[Neverwinter Wood]]. The ruins are about fifty miles northwest of Phandalin, and she provides directions so the characters can easily find the place.
