# Conyberry

## Story so far

According to [[Sister Garaele]], the town of Conyberry was sacked by barbarians years ago and now lies in ruins. It is situated right on top of [[Triboar Trail]], and lies to the northeast of Phandalin.

Sister Garaele revealed that the banshee Agatha's lair is near the Conyberry ruins
