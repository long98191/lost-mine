# Wyvern Tor

## Story so far

This cliff is a prominent landmark in the rugged hills northeast of the Sword Mountains, and is easily visible from twenty miles away. People traveling along the [[Triboar Trail]] in the vicinity of [[Conyberry]] catch glimpses of Wyvern Tor to the south as they go. The tor was formerly the home of a large and dangerous nest of wyverns, but a band of bold adventurers dealt with the monsters years ago. Though the wyverns never returned, other creatures lair here from time to time.

According to [[Harbin Wester]], orc raiders is increasingly rampant in the roads near this cliff.
