# Sleeping Giant Inn

## Story so far

The Sleeping Giant tap house lies at the east side of Phandalin. Compared to [[Stonehill Inn]] which is a quaint but cozy space for socializing and relaxing, the Sleeping Giant is a small, dilapidated tap house that looks almost run down to the ground. Few in town dares to give it but a glance, since no good things ever came with it.

A number of shady figures are seen frequenting the tap house. Noone says it explicitly, but everyone knows those are the [[Redbrands Ruffians]], always ready to harrass the lives of the good people of Phandalin.
